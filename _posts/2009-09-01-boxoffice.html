Boxoffice.es is the most popular web site I have delivered, with 25,000 monthly visits. The author needed a simple way to publish articles and reports on a daily basis. After evaluating different cms options, Wordpress was eventually determined as most adapt server-side component for the web site.

Another requirement by the customer was to have the look-and-feel of an online magazine, rather than that of a blog. This was accomplished by means of an original theme written in php right for this site.

To allow the author to include linked references to each movie, studio, genre and year throughout their articles, I'm currently developing wp-taquilla, a Wordpress plugin that improves the admin interface enabling users to add, manage and fill tables in the database directly from the browser.

The front-end is the part I'm most proud of: nice look-and-feel for both the web site and the newsletter, integration with Twitter, Facebook and iGoogle, valid xhtml strict and css code, A-level speed performance (source: y! slow) and the honour of being the top rated website in Spain for usability, accessibility and SEO (source: Qualidator).
